What spaces are you most excited about?

Extended Reality (XR, VR, AR, and MR) moves the medium away from the screen and into 3D space, which humans love to play with!

Deep learning is a huge asset for automation and procedural object generation. This is especially good for content pipelines at game studios.

Mid-to-large size companies spend a lot on hiring and training. XR applications can offset those costs while also offering better results.
