  • Extended Reality

    Extended reality (XR, VR, AR, and MR) moves the medium away from the screen and into 3D space, which is where our brains were meant to play. We've crossed a threshhold; XR is finally within reach.

  • Deep Learning & automation

    I've made a habit of automating development workflows where possible; machine learning can augment this approach significantly, and this is a core pillar of my approach to the tech stack. Automate all the things!

  • Mindset & Relationships

    People sometimes ask me: What is the most valuable thing you've learned as a founder? Rather than tech, product, or business, I say that cultivating a growth mindset and investing in relationships are the keys to success. (Oh, and a whole lot of grit!)

What are you best at?

  • Designing a fun-to-touch 3D experience.
  • Envisioning the company from end-to-end.
  • Executing on-time deliverables.

What roles can you do well?

As a founder I've had to be "good enough" at a lot of things, and I'll learn whatever is needed to execute on high level tasks. What drives me is a focus on the end user experience and building a successful business.
  • Here are some skills I picked up along my way, in order of ascending competence:
  • Marketing & Sales
  • Full stack web dev
  • Automate asset pipelines
  • Executive strategy
  • Project management
  • 3D interaction design & programming

Where do you see yourself in five years?

  • I will be doing what I love and creating the most value I can.
  • I will be a founding member of a 10 person XR company.
  • I will (part-time) run an executive consulting firm with a dozen clients who come to me for advice and technical pivots.
  • I will improve my mindset and relationships -- and have a high skill in fostering a positive work environment, where each member puts in their all and achieves self actualization in their role.

What are you looking for?

I'm currently building a company in the XR space for corporate training.

More generally, I'm looking for fun, challenging, work with a fun, competent team! Ideally as a contractor, advisor or consultant, as I would like to leverage myself as highly as possible to maximize my impact -- although I will consider full-time work for the right fit (probably a startup).

What industries do you work in?

  • Virtual / Augmented Reality
  • Biotech, especially nanobots and motors
  • Data visualization
  • 3D experience design, games, and simulations
  • Deep learning

Why do you work?

I love to have fun, and I love playing with fun toys. When I build something, I share in the joy of the experience.
