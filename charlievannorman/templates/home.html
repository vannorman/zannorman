Conversation with Charlie simulator (version 1.0)

What excites you?

Extended Reality (XR, VR, AR, and MR) moves the medium away from the screen and into 3D space, which is where our brains were meant to play. We've crossed a threshhold; XR is finally within reach!

On the development pipeline side, I'm very bullish on augmenting or replacing workflows with automation and machine learning.

What is the most valuable thing you've learned as a founder?

Cultivating a growth mindset, positive attitude, and strong personal relationships.

What skills do you have?

As a founder I have a passion for creating amazing experiences for the end user, and empowering my team to do so as well. I've picked up some useful skills in this pursuit:
  • Company vision
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Full stack development
  • Project management
  • 3D game programming

What are you looking for?

I am available for consulting work in the 3D application space, including traditional video games, virtual reality, and augmented reality applications.

I am in the early stages of building an XR development studio, and am filling out the team. Contact me to learn more.

What industries interest you?

  • Virtual / Augmented Reality
  • High fidelity aptitude assessment apps
  • Biotech, especially nanobots and motors
  • Data visualization
  • Deep learning

  • Climbing, bouldering, hiking
  • Martial arts, especially weapons (I am a total amateuro tho!)
  • Snowboarding, skiing, surfing, skateboarding
  • Machine learning / Deep learning
